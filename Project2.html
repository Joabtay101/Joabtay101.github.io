Caerdydd Dragon - A Puzzle Platformer

Overview

Caerdydd Dragon is a 2D puzzle platformer inspired by the cultural heritage of Wales. The game features the iconic Welsh dragon as the protagonist, navigating through levels set within Cardiff Castle, collecting daffodils along the way. Developed in Unity using C#, the game emphasizes accessibility, smooth movement mechanics, and an engaging difficulty curve while showcasing a strong sense of place and identity tied to its setting. The project was a team effort, with all four members contributing to game mechanics, story, and overall design.

Project Goals

  • Engaging Gameplay: Provide an intuitive yet challenging experience that balances accessibility with increasing difficulty.
  • Cultural Representation: Capture the essence of Welsh culture through environment design and character choice.
  • Technical Execution: Develop a polished platforming system with well-integrated movement, camera, and enemy interactions.
  • Replayability: Encourage players to return through collectibles and refined mechanics.

Key Features

  • Smooth Movement & Camera Mechanics: Player movement was designed for accessibility, with intuitive controls tested for ease of use.
  • Progressive Difficulty & Level Design: Two distinct levels, each introducing a new mechanic.
  • Unique Gameplay Elements: Wind Zones, Collectibles, and Enemy Interactions.

Technical Specifications

  • Git Workflow: Master branch holds the final submission, development branch for ongoing work, and feature branches for isolated implementations.
  • Key Algorithms: Singleton Pattern, Input Handling, UI Animation System, Camera Control, and Enemy AI.
